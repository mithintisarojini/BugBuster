What is Sliding Window?

Sliding window reduces unnecessary computations by maintaining a "window" over part of the array/string and shifting it intelligently.

Use Cases

  • Finding maximum/minimum subarray sum of size k.
  • Longest substring without repeating characters.
  • Subarray problems with constraints.
